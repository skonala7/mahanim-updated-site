What We Do

Shrink the bytes, keep the brilliance.

At Mahanim, our focus is on creating IP assets to compress image and video content order of magnitude better than current state of art. Our solutions can be used with content on-cloud, on-prem, and on handheld devices by customers providing services in the areas of CSP, OTT, Video surveillance, Multimedia content delivery, and personal media content creation.

Our name embodies our philosophy. Maha represents the vast, expansive landscape of modern digital media, while anim signifies the tiny, atomic-level details that make each piece of content unique. By blending the power of deep learning with next generation AI driven methods, we transform traditional multimedia processing into an art form.

We ensure that whether it's a high-resolution image or a dynamic video, every element is handled with the highest fidelity, preserving the brilliance of your source material.

Experience the future of multimedia, where immense scale meets atomic precision.
